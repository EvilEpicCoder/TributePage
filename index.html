Company history

  • 1985 - Brett Sperry and Louis Castle founded Westwood Studios, Inc. in Las Vegas, Nevada
  • 1992 - was merged with Virgin Interactive (company not exist anymore)
  • 1998-2003 - was bought from Virgin Interactive by Electronic Arts
